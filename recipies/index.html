Tonkatsu (Japanese Pork Cutlet)

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Servings: 2

Instructions

  1. Season the pork chops with salt and pepper.
  2. Set up three shallow dishes: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with panko breadcrumbs.
  3. Dredge each pork chop in flour, then dip in egg, and finally coat with panko breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Fry the breaded pork chops for about 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Drain on a wire rack or paper towels.
  7. Slice the tonkatsu into strips and serve with tonkatsu sauce and shredded cabbage.

Notes

For best results, use pork loin chops that are about 1/2 inch thick. You can also use chicken breast for a variation called "chicken katsu".
